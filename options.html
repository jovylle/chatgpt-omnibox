How to Use

🔍 Default Search

Type chat hello world to search your default AI service.

🎯 Specific AI Service

Type chat gpt: hello world or chat claude: explain this to use a specific AI.

🖱️ Context Menu

Right-click selected text and choose "Search AI for..." to search with your default service.

⚡ Quick Access

Click the extension icon to open your default AI service directly.
